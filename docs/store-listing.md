## Description (long)

### English
VoiceStudyFlow turns your own PDF books into a calm, focused study experience.
Add a PDF, open it, and listen in a clear, natural human voice — in Arabic or
English — instead of tiring robotic narration. A moving magnifier follows along
as it reads, so it's easy to stay on the line.

**What you can do**
• Read & listen — upload any PDF and hear it in a natural voice, with speed
  control and section-by-section navigation.
• Smart study plan — set your daily minutes and the app spreads the book's pages
  across days automatically, with rest days.
• Magnifier reading — a large, moving lens centers each word as it's read; rotate
  to landscape for an even bigger view.
• Pronunciation & translation — accurate diacritized pronunciation and instant
  translation.
• AI study tools — one tap for a page summary, practice questions, flashcards, a
  mind map, or a presentation.
• Focus & tracking — a focus timer (Pomodoro), day streaks, and focus mode that
  reduces distractions while you read.
• Your library, organized — folders, archive, and a study syllabus with quizzes
  and audio summaries.

Your books and progress are saved privately to your account and kept completely
separate from everyone else. You're responsible for the content you upload.

Designed and developed by Nahla · Made in Riyadh.

## App Privacy / Data safety — data types to declare

Declare these as **collected, linked to the user, not used for tracking, not
sold**:
- **Contact info:** email address (account).
- **User content:** files/documents (uploaded PDFs), other user content
  (highlights, notes, study data).
- **Identifiers:** user ID (account).
- **Diagnostics:** none collected by us beyond what the platform provides.

Processing purposes: **App functionality** (and **account management**). Not used
for advertising or tracking. Third-party processors: Supabase, ElevenLabs,
Google Cloud Vision, AI assistant provider (content sent only to fulfill the
requested feature).
